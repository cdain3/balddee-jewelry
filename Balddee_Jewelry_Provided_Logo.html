About Us

I'm Dashuna, but most folks know me as Balddee. I'm proud to introduce myself as the proprietor and creative mind behind Beads By Balddee.

Why did I venture into this business, you might wonder? Well, crafting has been my passion since childhood. From collecting to designing, the world of jewelry has always held a special allure for me. It was this deep-rooted love that led me to establish myself as a bracelet designer back in 2022.

Understanding the essence of uniqueness in jewelry, I've made customization and handcrafting the cornerstones of my service. Every piece you see in my store is a testament to my dedication, personally designed and meticulously crafted by yours truly. I find joy in creating a diverse array of bracelets, drawing inspiration from a variety of materials, with a special penchant for jade, crystal, and an assortment of natural stones sourced mainly from Myanmar.

For me, sincerity and skill are paramount. I firmly believe that it's the authenticity and craftsmanship that truly captivate people's hearts. So, come on in and explore the world of Balddee Jewelry, where every piece tells a story of passion and dedication.
